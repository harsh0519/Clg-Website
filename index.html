The Vidhyarise Institute of Higher Education(A Unit of GIHE EDUCATION & CONSULTANCY PVT. LTD.), Noida has been established in 2003. The institute since then is working in the education industry to bridge the gap between the higher education and the students. The objective of the institute is to promote the education across country. The institute is working with many UGC, AICTE, DEB, PCI, and many other reputed Universities of India. As the mission of the institute is to make education available for everyone and anyone so they are working for regular and distance education courses. The institute also supports students with the in-house classes and the material and motivational support.

The goodies you will get from the institute are-

  • excellent support team
  • Student E-portal
  • job assistance
